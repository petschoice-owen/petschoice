Spike's has been providing high quality, nutritious hedghehog food for over 20 years. They are proud to say that since we at Pets Choice acquired the brand in May 2017, they have developed and improved the brand and product offering. Spike's produce and manufacture naturally nutritious complementary wild hedgehog food in Blackburn, Lancashire. Their goal is to keep hedgehogs happy and healthy due to their declining population by providing food that's naturally nutritious. At Spike's, they value the health and well-being of wild hedgehogs, which is why it is their priority to help facilitate a flourishing future for hedgehogs. Spike's mission is to educate the Great British public on how they can help hedgehogs in the wild.
