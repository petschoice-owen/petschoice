Our Ethos

We've been around for over 135 years and that's down to the hard work and integrity of our people. We care for each other as much as we take care in our products.

We are a team

We work as part of one big team. We achieve more when we collaborate, support each other, and work together. We empower each other and value each other's work.

We have integrity in our nature

We do what is right and are always honest. We act ethically and we follow through on the commitments we make.

We respect everyone

We have respect for our colleagues, our customers, and our environment. We embrace diversity and value people's differences. We respect each other's time and the importance of every area of our business.

We have passion for all we do

We keep passion at the heart of everything we do, we encourage creativity and believe that it's our passion that drives our business forward.

We take pride in our achievements

We take pride in the work that we do and are proud of the work of our colleagues. We value everyone's contribution and celebrate achievements as individuals and as part of a team.

We strive for success in all that we do

We want to succeed in everything that we do by working hard and to the best of our abilities, no matter how big or how small.
