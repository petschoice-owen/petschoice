Our People

Meet The Team

At the heart of our pack are the incredible people who make everything possible! We're a close-knit family dedicated to bringing joy to pets and their owners.

Our passion for providing the best for our furry friends fuels everything we do, making it a joy to work here. We believe in our brands, innovation, company ethos, and ourselves! Despite our long and successful history, we never take our achievements for granted.

We know that progress comes from questioning and improving our processes and decisions. Every team member is encouraged to use their initiative, inspire their colleagues, and make innovation an everyday part of the Pets Choice family.

Here's what some of our team members have to say about working with us:

Hey, I'm Jenny, the finance assistant here at Pets Choice. I joined the company back in 2022 as a credit controller as I was looking to develop myself professionally. After achieving fantastic results in my first year, the stars aligned (again!) and I was offered a newly created position within the Finance department due to the company growing. This included a 5-year training plan to become a qualified management accountant, incorporating MT Level 3 &4 along with CGMA- it was a dream come true! The support and guidance I've received in my new role has been phenomenal and it truly feels like the sky is the limit It's been life changing working for a company that invests so much into their employees.

Jenny Smith Finance Assistant

Pets Choice has significantly contributed to my career development since my graduation from university. Beginning my journey as a quality control (QC) analyst and progressing to my recent acceptance into training as a qualified person (QP), Pets Choice has been instrumental in fostering my professional growth and success.

Gus Hold QC Analyst Patton

I began my journey at Pets Choice in 2020 during lockdown and just before the UK and EU finalised the Trade and Cooperation Agreement in January 2021. The TCA significantly overhauled the UK's trading relationship with the EU, which meant I had to reshape our entire export operation for our 45 international distributors.

Brexit has since been an evolving challenge for UK importers and exporters, requiring businesses to continually adapt to new or reinterpreted legislation on a weekly basis and so we've expanded the export team by adding two new members to support with increased demand and the growing complexity of our supply chain.

In October 2023, I began a three-year degree course in Supply Chain Management, which I have since been studying alongside my role. In January 2024, my team took on certain supply chain functions from the Blackburn procurement team help to strengthen our business's position on new and evolving import controls and shipping legislation from Europe and the Far East.

Over the past four years at Pets Choice, my role has evolved significantly, and I thoroughly enjoy the journey. Trading in a post-Brexit world can be challenging and you often have to find creative solutions to navigate the red tape, and that is what I enjoy and like to think I'm quite good at it.

The business has supported my growth through several industry training courses over the years and allowed me the freedom to develop our international trade processes. I truly enjoy working at Pets Choice and appreciate the support and collaboration from the teams around me.

Robert Foy International Trade Compliance Manager

I am thoroughly enjoying my time at Pets Choice, where I am continually enhancing my skills. The friendly team and the company's exciting innovations in the pet industry are invaluable. It is a privilege to supply reputable brands such as Webbox, AniMate, and Vitakraft to our diverse nationwide customer base. I look forward to continuing to drive success through business support and sales.

Megan Watson Pet Trade National Account Manager

When Pets Choice acquired Bob Martin, I had been working in the Pet healthcare industry for 25 years, so this is my 30th year working in this industry. I started working as a warehouse operative and have progressed over the years to my current role as Product Supply Manager. As I'd never worked anywhere other than Bob Martin, working for a new company was a bit daunting but I must say that the transition was absolutely fine. I really enjoy getting involved with all the new projects and as part of my day to day tasks, ensuring we fulfil customer orders. I'm proud to work for such a progressive company with such great people.

Sam Allen Product Supply Manager
