Here at Vitakraft we offer a wide range of delicious foods and tasty treats for dogs, cats, birds, and small animals. We use only the best ingredients to create healthy wholesome recipes and for over 180 years our philosophy has been "for the love of pets" so our number one priority is to develop and produce innovative, high-quality products to ensure that all your pets receive the perfect diet.

Irresistibly delicious food across the full range of products

Now an international brand, Vita kraft began as a small pet food shop in a small village in Germany

A range of over 2,500 products that includes food, snacks, pet care, and hygiene

Dietary concepts that have been created based around scientific studies into animal eating patterns in the wild

Food ranges for dogs, cats, rodents, birds, fish, reptiles, hedgehogs, and ferrets

Incredible levels of innovation that continue to transform the global pet care market
