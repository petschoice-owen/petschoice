Hatchwell Company Ltd was established in 1933 in Preston to supply a liquid which softened hen eggs; chicks could easily peck their way through the egg shell after it was softened, thus they would 'Hatch well!

Since then we have added many more products into the range, including a wide array of dog, cat, small animal, equestrian and bird products. The majority of our products are currently manufactured in the UK and are therefore of the highest quality.

It is our aim to produce quality treats, accessories and healthcare products to enhance the quality of every pet's life. As part of our repertoire, we can also supply products as own label ranges.
