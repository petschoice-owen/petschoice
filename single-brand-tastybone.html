TastyBone are the UK's leading manufacturer of 100% virgin nylon super tough chew toys and are fast becoming a recognised worldwide brand, exported to over 30 countries. Designed, manufactured, and packaged within UK-based factories, TastyBone ensures complete control over all areas of the manufacturing process, guaranteeing the highest standards. The chew toys come in an array of flavours, sizes, and shapes all uniquely infused with flavour that lasts the lifetime of the chew toy.
