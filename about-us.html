Our Community

As a leading UK pet food manufacturer with a worldwide customer base, we take our responsibilities to the environment and our local community very seriously. We're conscious of the sustainability and environmental impact of every part of our manufacturing and distribution processes, including sourcing raw ingredients, sustainable energy usage during manufacturing and transportation, and even the packaging we use.

"Blackburn, and the people there, are very close to my heart, which is why we support the Blackburn Youth Zone and work alongside many other charities. Over the years we have seen first-hand the positive impact that Blackburn Youth Zone has made in the local community. The safe space and services they offer are invaluable and their support for young people enables them to flourish." Tony Raeburn, Pets Choice CEO
